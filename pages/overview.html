Services Overview

The Municipal Health Info System offers a comprehensive range of digital services designed to support community health management and improve healthcare outcomes.

Our key services include:

  • Patient Registration: A simplified digital form to enroll new patients, ensuring that basic personal and health-related data are accurately recorded and securely stored.
  • Appointment Scheduling: Allows patients to book appointments for consultations, checkups, and vaccinations to reduce wait times and overcrowding at health centers.
  • Vaccination Tracking: Monitors vaccination records, especially for children, pregnant women, and vulnerable groups. Includes alerts for upcoming vaccine schedules.
  • Medical Records Management: Provides authorized staff with secure access to patients’ medical history, diagnoses, and treatments—anytime, anywhere.
  • Health Reports & Statistics: Automatically generates barangay-level health data, helping administrators to identify trends, outbreaks, and service needs.
  • Program Updates: Shares information about upcoming health programs such as feeding initiatives, wellness checks, and medical missions.

All services are designed to comply with local government health protocols and data privacy laws to ensure that citizen health data remains confidential and protected.
